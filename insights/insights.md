# Project Insights

This document summarizes key business insights derived from the SQL analysis.

---

## 1. Monthly Revenue Trends
- Revenue shows clear month-to-month variation, indicating seasonal or campaign-driven effects.
- Tracking revenue at a monthly level helps management identify growth periods and slowdowns.

---

## 2. Revenue by Department
- Revenue is concentrated in a small number of departments.
- This suggests certain departments are key drivers of overall business performance.

---

## 3. Employee Performance
- Sales performance varies significantly across employees.
- A small group of top-performing employees contributes a large share of total revenue.

---

## 4. Top Employee per Department
- Each department has a clear top-performing employee.
- This insight can support performance reviews, incentive allocation, and training decisions.

---

## 5. Customer Spending Behavior
- A small number of customers account for a disproportionately large share of total revenue.
- Customer retention and loyalty programs should prioritize these high-value customers.
